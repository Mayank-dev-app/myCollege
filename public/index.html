Brife Introduction

I.P. (P.G.) College, Campus-2, Bulandshahr is situated at NH-91 Dariyapur Bulandshahr. Having three storey building, well equiped with excellent infrastructure and high-tech facilities. This Campus-2 is originated and founded by I.P. College Campus-I, Bulandshahr is situated near siyana bus stand Bulandshahr which has grant-in-aid courses. The Parents Society of both campus is "Ishwar Dayal Parsandi Devi Vidhya Prasarni Sabha" a charitable society entitled to receive donations under section '80G' of Income Tax. I.P. College campus-2 is covered in 24730 sqm land. For Dept. of Teacher Education total land area 10000 sqm. is earmarked and remaining land area i.e. 14730 sqm. is earmarked for other courses. Total built up area of I.P. College, Campus-2 is 9172.49 sqm. certified and approved by architect and BDA.
